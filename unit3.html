HTML & CSS

Hyper Text Markup Language (HTML) is the most widely-used markup language, and is used to develop the structure of web-pages; it was created by Tim-Berners Lee in 1991. Cascading Style Sheets (CSS) is the most widely-used style sheet/design language, and is used to design, style, and decorate the appearance of web pages; it was created W3C in 1996. The two languages are the the most widely used languages in the web-industry.
